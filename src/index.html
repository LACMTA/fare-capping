Metro is proposing Fare Changes

How Will Fare Changes Impact You?

Metro is proposing a simpler fare system where no rider ever overpays, and our most frequent riders who rely on the system pay less when they use TAP.

Key Fare Changes

  • Key Fare Changes Regular fare for a single ride will cost $2 instead of $1.75.
  • Transfers will no longer be free
  • 1-day, 7-day, and 30-day passes are going away.
  • Daily and weekly fare caps when you use a TAP card.
