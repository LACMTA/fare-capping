Proposed Metro Fare Changes

How Will Fare Changes Impact You?

Metro is proposing a more simple fare structure where no rider ever overpays and our most frequent riders who rely on the system pay less with TAP.

Key Fare Changes

  • Regular Fare for a single ride will cost $2 instead of $1.75.
  • Transfers will no longer be free
  • Metro 1-day, 7-day, and 30-day passes are going away.
  • Daily ($6) and weekly ($20) fare caps will activate when you use a TAP card.
