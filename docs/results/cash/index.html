Get TAP for lower fares

  • Qualify for reduced fare and each Metro trip would only cost $1.

  • With reduced fare capping you never pay more than $3 a day or $8 a week. Every ride after would be FREE.

  • With regular fare capping you never pay more than $6 a day or $20 a week.

Proposed Fare Changes

  1. Regular fare will cost $2 instead of $1.75
  2. Transfers will no longer be free
  3. Metro 1-day, 7-day, and 30-day passes will go away

What is Fare Capping?

A pay-as-you-go fare payment model that ensures customers only pay for the rides they take and never overpay.
